About me

I'm Jiwei(Jay) Yao, currently a Ph.D. student at University of Utah, advised by Prof. Kody Powell and Prof. Tao Gao. My research is focusing on two parts. The first part focusing on optimizing the operation of the battery energy storage system with a physics-based battery model to capture the interaction between operation and degradation behaviors. Another research pillar of mine is developing physics-based machine learning methods to predict the lifetime of battery with limited data.

Previously, I got my MS degree in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering from Cornell University advised by Prof. Fengqi You. I received my BS in Chemical Engineering from Case Western Reserve University in 2018.
